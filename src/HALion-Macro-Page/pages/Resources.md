# Resources

There are three types of resources available on macro pages. These are bitmaps, fonts, and sections. Resources are the most basic objects and are used by controls to display graphics, animations, or text.

* [Bitmap](./Bitmap.md)
* [Font](./Font.md)
* [Section](./Section.md)
